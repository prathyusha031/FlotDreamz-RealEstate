Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I list my property on Flot Dreamz?

To list your property, sign up for an account, navigate to the 'Sell' section, and fill in the required details with images.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other online payment methods.

3. Can I cancel my property listing?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your listing anytime from your dashboard.

4. Do you charge any commission?

No, we do not charge any commission on property listings.
