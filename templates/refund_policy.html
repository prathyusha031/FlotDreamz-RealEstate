Refund Policy

At Flot Dreamz, we strive to provide the best service and ensure customer satisfaction. Below are the details of our refund policy:

1. Eligibility for Refund

Refunds are applicable under the following conditions:

  • If the purchased property listing is removed before the agreed duration.
  • If payment was deducted twice due to a system error.
  • If a technical issue prevents access to purchased features.

2. Non-Refundable Services

Refunds are not available for the following:

  • Completed real estate transactions.
  • Service charges related to property promotions.
  • Processing fees for consultations.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@flotdreamz.com with your transaction details.
  2. Our team will verify the claim within 7-10 business days.
  3. If approved, the refund will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
